"Raise the Roof for Audrey" is a fundraising campaign associated with habitat for Humanity Tuscaloosa. Through The University of Alabama, a team of Master's of Marketing students, are working to raise $50,000 to build Audrey Harris a new home. Audrey has lived in Tuscaloosa her whole life. The house itself is 119 years old and is no longer a safe structure in which to live.

Check out their webiste and Facebook page for more information. Donations are still being accepted: http://raisetheroofforaudrey.wordpress.com/
